【Responsive Reading啟應讀經】Psalm詩篇 8
（L）1  Lord, our Lord, how majestic is your name in all the earth! You have set your glory in the heavens.
（R）2 Through the praise of children and infants you have established a stronghold against your enemies, to silence the foe and the avenger.
（L）3 When I consider your heavens, the work of your fingers, the moon and the stars, which you have set in place,
（R）4 what is mankind that you are mindful of them, human beings that you care for them?
（L）5 You have made them a little lower than the angels and crowned them with glory and honor.
（R）6 You made them rulers over the works of your hands; you put everything under their feet:
（L）7 all flocks and herds, and the animals of the wild,
（R）8 the birds in the sky, and the fish in the sea, all that swim the paths of the seas.
（T）9 Lord, our Lord, how majestic is your name in all the earth!
（領）1耶和華我們的主啊！你的名在全地是多麼威嚴，你把你的榮美彰顯在天上。
（眾）2 因你仇敵的緣故，你從小孩和嬰兒的口中，得著了讚美，使仇敵和報仇的無話可說。
（領）3 我觀看你手所造的天，和你所安放的月亮和星星。
（眾）4 啊！人算甚麼，你竟記念他？世人算甚麼，你竟眷顧他？
（領）5 你使他比天使低微一點，卻賜給他榮耀尊貴作冠冕。
（眾）6 你叫他管理你手所造的，把萬物都放在他的腳下，
（領）7 就是所有的牛羊、田間的走獸、
（眾）8 空中的飛鳥、海裡的魚，和海裡游行的水族。
（合）9 耶和華我們的主啊！你的名在全地是多麼威嚴。
